How to set a task's priority
Four quadrants. Urgent & important → do now (deadline + real consequence). Important, not urgent → schedule it; this is where the forward work lives. Urgent, not important → minimise or delegate. Neither → drop it. Most things that feel urgent are only urgent — be honest about what's actually important.
